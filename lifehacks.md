# Top 5 Life Hacks for College Students

College life can be hectic, but these simple life hacks will make it easier and more fun.

## 1. Plan Your Day
Use a planner or phone app to schedule classes, assignments, and study sessions. Planning saves time and reduces stress.

## 2. Use the Pomodoro Technique
Study in 25-minute focused intervals with 5-minute breaks. This boosts concentration and prevents burnout.

## 3. Keep a Healthy Snack Stash
Keep fruits, nuts, or protein bars in your bag. Healthy snacks help maintain energy during long classes.

## 4. Make Study Groups
Collaborate with friends to discuss tricky topics. Study groups make learning interactive and fun.

## 5. Digital Declutter
Organize your computer files, apps, and emails regularly. A clean digital workspace reduces distractions.

**Conclusion:**  
Small daily habits make college life smoother and more productive.
